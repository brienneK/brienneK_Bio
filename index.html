Brienne's Bio

Brienne is a human rights activist turned coder. After getting her MA in International Peace and Conflict Resolution from American Univeristy and working in the nonprofit world for 5 years, she wanted to add some techincal skills to her resume and so turned to tech itself. As a student in MITxPro's Full Stack Development Course, Brienne has been learning how to use JavaScript, CSS, and HTML to dynamically code for an interactive web experience. After completing this course, Brienne intends to bring her new devleopment skills back to the realm of human rights to support nonprofits' initatives online.
